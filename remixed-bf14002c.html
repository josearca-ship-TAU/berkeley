Heritage & Philosophy

The
Berkeley
Standard

I

Mayfair Provenance

Berkeley draws its name and sensibility from London’s most composed quarter — a district synonymous with understated wealth and inherited taste.

II

Flavor as Refinement

We occupy a deliberate space between characterless premium and aggressive flavored mass-market. Flavor, used with precision, is the mark of a sophisticated house.

III

Selective Distribution

Berkeley is not available everywhere. Duty-free corridors, curated travel retail, and select specialty trade only. Scarcity is not strategy — it is consequence.

“There is a customer in every major city in the world who has grown tired of being sold to. Berkeley is made for them.”

Berkeley House, London

The brand emerges from a conviction that the luxury tobacco segment has been left uncontested — dominated at one end by legacy houses resting on heritage, and at the other by flavored products competing on novelty.

Berkeley enters the gap: a brand with credible European character, a coherent visual identity, and a product philosophy rigorous enough to survive the scrutiny of a serious buyer.
